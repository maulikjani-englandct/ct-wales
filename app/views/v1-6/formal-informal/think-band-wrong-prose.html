I think my Council Tax band is wrong

This page is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

There are two ways you can request a review of your Council Tax band if you think it is wrong:

  • formal review
  • informal review
Compare a formal review and an informal review
Formal review Informal review
What it is A VOA caseworker will carry out a formal review of your Council Tax band. This is also known as a ‘Informal Challenge’ and can be made at any point.
What it's for For challenging your Council Tax band. To check whether your Council Tax band is correct.
When you can ask for it
  • If you have been paying Council Tax on your home for less than six months
  • If the VOA has changed your Council Tax band in the last six months

You can also make a proposal to tell us that your home has changed. For example:

  • the home has been demolished
  • it has been divided into new homes
  • it has been merged into a single home
  • there has been a physical change in your local area that has affected the value of your home
 You can ask for a band review at any time.
How long it takes Up to x weeks Up to x weeks
Evidence required You do not need to provide any evidence. However, any information you give us will help the VOA understand why you think your home should be in a different band. The VOA will only review the band if you provide supporting evidence.
Examples of evidence
  • addresses of similar homes in a lower band than yours
  • the price that your home or similar homes sold for between 1 April 2001 and 31 March 2005
  • addresses of up to 5 similar homes in a lower band than yours (similar in age, style, design, size and type)
  • these properties should be in a nearby street or estate if you live in a town or city, or within 10 miles if you live in the countryside
  • the price that your home or similar homes sold for between 1 April 2001 and 31 March 2005
Decision There are 3 possible decisions that can be made:
  • your home stays in the same band
  • your home is moved into a lower band
  • your home is moved into a higher band
 There are 3 possible decisions that can be made:
  • your home stays in the same band
  • your home is moved into a lower band
  • your home is moved into a higher band
After a decision has been made You have 4 months after the date of the decision to appeal to the Valuation Tribunal Wales if you are not satisfied with the outcome. The decision is final, and you cannot ask for another review.
