Council Tax band review

You can ask the VOA (Valuation Office Agency) to review the Council Tax band of your home.

You will need to tell us why you think the band is wrong and you may be asked for more details.

You can request a review of your council tax band if:

  • you became the new taxpayer within the last 6 months
  • the council tax band has changed in the last 6 months
  • the local area has physically changed

You can also request a review if something has changed with the property. For example:

  • its been demolished
  • its partly used for business
  • its been split into multiple properties or merged into one

If you request a review for one of these reasons and don't agree with the outcome you can appeal the decision.

Reviewing the band for any other reason

If none of the above apply, we will only review your band if you have strong supporting evidence that it is wrong.

If you don't give us strong enough evidence that the band is wrong, we will reject your request for a band review.

You won't have any legal rights to appeal the outcome of a review.
