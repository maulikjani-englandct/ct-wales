Asking for a formal review

You have told us that there have been changes made to your home or significant changes made in the local area that affect the value of your home.

This means that you can ask for a formal review of your Council Tax band. This is also known as a ‘Formal Challenge’ or ‘Making a Proposal’.

You can change your selection if this option is not right for you.

How a formal review works

What it is You can ask the Valuation Office Agency (VOA) to review the Council Tax band your home is in.
What it's for You can ask for a formal review if you think that your home is in the wrong Council Tax band.
When you can ask for a formal review You can ask for a formal review if there have been changes made to your home or the local area. For example:
  • all or part of your home has been demolished
  • it has been split into new homes
  • it has been merged into a larger, single home
  • something in the local area has changed that has affected the value of your home
How long it takes Up to X weeks
Evidence required You do not need to provide any evidence. However, any information you give us will help the VOA understand why you think your home should be in a different band.
Examples of evidence
  • addresses of similar homes in a lower band than yours.
  • the price that your home or similar homes sold for between 1 April 2001 and 31 March 2005
Decision There are 4 possible decisions that can be made:
  • your home stays in the same band
  • your home is moved into a lower band
  • your home is moved into a higher band
  • your home is deleted from the Council Tax list
After a decision has been made You have 4 months after the date of the decision to appeal to the Valuation Tribunal Wales if you are not satisfied with the outcome.

Gofyn am adolygiad ffurfiol

Rwyt ti wedi dweud wrthym fod newidiadau wedi’u gwneud i dy gartref, neu bod newidiadau mawr wedi bod yn yr ardal leol, sy’n effeithio ar werth dy gartref.

Mae hyn yn golygu dy fod yn gallu gofyn am adolygiad ffurfiol o dy fand Treth Gyngor. Enw arall am hyn ydy ‘Her Ffurfiol’ neu ‘Gwneud Cynnig’.

Rwyt ti’n gallu newid dy ddewis os dyw’r opsiwn yma ddim yn iawn i ti.

Sut mae adolygiad ffurfiol yn gweithio

Beth yw e Rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn i Asiantaeth y Swyddfa Brisio (VOA) adolygu’r band Treth Gyngor mae dy gartref ynddo.
Ar gyfer beth yw e Rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn am adolygiad ffurfiol os wyt ti’n meddwl bod dy gartref yn y band Treth Gyngor anghywir.
Pryd rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn am adolygiad ffurfiol Rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn am adolygiad ffurfiol os mae newidiadau wedi’u gwneud i dy gartref neu’r ardal leol. Er enghraifft:
  • mae dy gartref cyfan neu ran ohono wedi’i ddymchwel
  • mae wedi’i rannu’n gartrefi newydd
  • mae wedi’i uno i fod yn un cartref mwy
  • mae rhywbeth yn yr ardal leol wedi newid ac wedi effeithio ar werth dy gartref
Pa mor hir mae’n cymryd Hyd at X wythnos
Y tystiolaeth sydd angen Dwyt ti ddim angen rhoi unrhyw dystiolaeth. Ond, bydd unrhyw wybodaeth rwyt ti’n ei roi yn helpu’r VOA i ddeall pam rwyt ti’n meddwl y dylai’r cartref fod mewn band gwahanol.
Enghreifftiau o dystiolaeth
  • cyfeiriadau cartrefi tebyg sydd mewn band is na dy un di.
  • y pris y gwerthwyd dy gartref neu gartrefi tebyg amdano rhwng 1 Ebrill 2001 a 31 Mawrth 2005.
Penderfyniad Mae 4 penderfyniad posib:
  • mae dy gartref yn aros yn yr un band
  • mae dy gartref yn symud i fand is
  • mae dy gartref yn symud i fand uwch
  • mae dy gartref yn cael ei ddileu o restr y Dreth Gyngor
Ar ôl y penderfyniad Os nad wyt ti’n hapus gyda’r canlyniad, mae gen ti 4 mis ar ôl dyddiad y penderfyniad i apelio i Dribiwnlys Prisio Cymru.
