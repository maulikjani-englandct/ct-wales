Tell us why you think the Council Tax band is wrong?

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "Your band will only be reviewed if you provide supporting evidence" }) }}

The evidence you provide will be looked at before a case is opened. You should give as much detail as possible.

What evidence can you include?

Evidence can include:

  • addresses of up to 5 homes, similar to yours in terms of type, size, age, layout and style, that are in a lower band. The homes should be in the same street or estate, or in the same village or area.
  • the price that your home or similar homes sold for between 1 April 2001 and 31 March 2005

How to provide your evidence

You can type your evidence in the text box. You can use up to 1,000 characters. You can also upload up to 5 files including PDFs, documents and photos on the next page.

Bydd y dystiolaeth rydych yn ei roi yn cael ei gwirio cyn bod achos yn cael ei agor. Dylech roi cymaint o fanylion â phosib.

What evidence can you include?

Gall tystiolaeth gynnwys:

  • cyfeiriadau hyd at 5 cartref sy’n debyg i’ch cartref o ran math, maint, oedran, cynllun a steil, sydd mewn band is. Dylai’r cartrefi fod ar yr un stryd neu stad, neu yn yr un pentref neu ardal.
  • y pris y gwerthwyd eich cartref neu gartrefi tebyg rhwng 1 Ebrill 2001 a 31 Mawrth 2005.

Sut i roi eich tystiolaeth

Rydych yn gallu teipio eich tystiolaeth yn y blwch testun. Mae gennych hyd at 1,000 o gymeriadau. Rydych yn gallu uwchlwytho hyd at 5 ffeil, gan gynnwys dogfennau PDF, dogfennau Word, a ffotograffau, ar y dudalen nesaf.
