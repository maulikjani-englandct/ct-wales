More information

What is Council Tax?

Council Tax is a tax on every home in England and Wales. The money raised by this tax helps pay for things like schools, rubbish collection, emergency services and social care.

Your local council works out how much tax is payable for each Council Tax band every year. They will send you your Council Tax bill and collect payment.

What is a Council Tax Band?

The Valuation Office Agency (VOA) puts every home into a group called a 'band'. These bands range from A to I in Wales, where band A is the lowest.

Find out more about band ranges.

The band your home is in is based on how much it was worth on 1 April 2003. The VOA is responsible for valuing your home and looks at what homes were selling for on that date, not what it is worth today.

Rhagor o wybodaeth

Beth yw Treth Gyngor?

Mae'r Dreth Gyngor yn dreth ar bob cartref yng Nghymru a Lloegr. Mae'r arian sy'n cael ei godi drwy'r dreth yn helpu i dalu am bethau fel ysgolion, casglu sbwriel, gwasanaethau brys a gofal cymdeithasol.

Mae dy gyngor lleol yn penderfynu faint o dreth sydd i'w thalu ar gyfer pob band Treth Gyngor bob blwyddyn. Nhw sy'n anfon dy fil Treth Gyngor atat ac yn casglu'r taliad.

Beth yw Band Treth Gyngor?

Mae Asiantaeth y Swyddfa Brisio (VOA) yn rhoi pob cartref mewn grŵp o'r enw 'band'. Yng Nghymru, mae'r bandiau yn mynd o A i I. Band A yw'r un isaf a Band I yw'r un uchaf.

Dysga fwy am yr ystodau bandiau.

Mae'r band mae dy gartref ynddo yn seiliedig ar ei werth ar 1 Ebrill 2003. Pan mae'r VOA yn prisio dy gartref, mae'n edrych ar faint roedd cartrefi'n cael eu gwerthu amdanyn nhw ar y dyddiad yna, nid gwerth y cartref heddiw.
