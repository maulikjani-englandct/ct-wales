Asking for a formal review

You have told us that you’ve been responsible for paying Council Tax on your home for less than 6 months.

This means that you can ask for a formal review of your Council Tax band. This is also known as a ‘Formal Challenge’ or ‘Making a Proposal’.

You can change your selection if this option is not right for you.

How a formal review works

What it is You can ask the Valuation Office Agency (VOA) to review the Council Tax band your home is in.
What it’s for You can ask for a formal review if you think that your home is in the wrong Council Tax band.
When you can ask for a formal review If you have been responsible for the Council Tax on your home for less than 6 months.
How long it takes Up to X weeks
Evidence required You do not need to provide any evidence. However, any information you give us will help the VOA understand why you think your home should be in a different band.
Examples of evidence
  • addresses of similar homes in a lower band than yours.
  • the price that your home or similar homes sold for between 1 April 2001 and 31 March 2005.
Decision There are 3 possible decisions that can be made:
  • your home stays in the same band
  • your home is moved into a lower band
  • your home is moved into a higher band
After a decision has been made You have 4 months after the date of the decision to appeal to the Valuation Tribunal Wales if you are not satisfied with the outcome.

Gofyn am adolygiad ffurfiol

Rwyt ti wedi dweud wrthym dy fod ti wedi bod yn gyfrifol am dalu Treth Gyngor ar dy gartref am lai na 6 mis.

Mae hyn yn golygu dy fod yn gallu gofyn am adolygiad ffurfiol o dy fand Treth Gyngor. Enw arall am hyn ydy ‘Her Ffurfiol’ neu ‘Gwneud Cynnig’.

Rwyt ti’n gallu newid dy ddewis os dyw’r opsiwn yma ddim yn iawn i ti.
