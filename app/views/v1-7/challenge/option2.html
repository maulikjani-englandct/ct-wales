Asking for an informal review

You have told us that you’ve been responsible for paying Council Tax on your home for more than 6 months.

This means that you can ask for an informal review of your Council Tax band. This is also known as a ‘Informal Challenge’ or ‘Band Review’.

You can change your selection if this option is not right for you.

How an informal review works

What it is You can ask the Valuation Office Agency (VOA) to review the Council Tax band your home is in.
What it's for You can ask for an informal review if you think that your home is in the wrong Council Tax band.
When you can ask for an informal review You can ask for a band review at any time.
How long it takes Up to X weeks
Evidence required The VOA will only review the band if you provide supporting evidence.
Examples of evidence
  • addresses of up to 5 similar homes in a lower band than yours. The homes should be the same as your home in terms of type, size, age and style.
  • the homes can be either in the same street or estate, or in the same village or area.
  • the price that your home or similar homes sold for between 1 April 2001 and 31 March 2005
Decision There are 3 possible decisions that can be made:
  • your home stays in the same band
  • your home is moved into a lower band
  • your home is moved into a higher band
After a decision has been made The decision is final, and you cannot ask for another review.

Gofyn am adolygiad anffurfiol

Rwyt ti wedi dweud wrthym dy fod ti wedi bod yn gyfrifol am dalu Treth Gyngor am dy gartref fwy na 6 mis.

Mae hyn yn meddwl dy fod yn gallu gofyn am adolygiad anffurfiol o dy fand Treth Gyngor. Enw arall am hyn ydy ‘Her Anffurfiol’ neu ‘Adolygiad Band’.

Rwyt ti’n gallu newid dy ddewis os dyw’r opsiwn yma ddim yn iawn i ti.

Yn ôl

Sut mae adolygiad anffurfiol yn gweithio

Beth yw e Rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn i Asiantaeth y Swyddfa Brisio (VOA) i adolygu band Treth Gyngor dy gartref.
Ar gyfer beth yw e Rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn am adolygiad anffurfiol os wyt ti’n meddwl bod dy gartref yn y band Treth Gyngor anghywir.
Pryd rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn am adolygiad anffurfiol Rwyt ti’n gallu gofyn am adolygiad band unrhyw bryd.
Pa mor hir mae’n cymryd Hyd at X wythnos
Y tystiolaeth sydd angen Bydd y VOA ond yn adolygu’r band os wyt ti’n rhoi tystiolaeth i’w gefnogi
Enghreifftiau o dystiolaeth
  • cyfeiriadau hyd at 5 cartref tebyg sydd mewn band is na dy fand di. Dylai’r cartrefi fod yr un fath â dy gartref di o ran math, maint, oedran a steil.
  • gall y cartrefi fod naill ai yn yr un stryd neu ystâd, neu yn yr un pentref neu ardal os wyt ti’n byw yng nghefn gwlad.
  • y pris y gwerthwyd dy gartref neu gartrefi tebyg amdano rhwng 1 Ebrill 2001 a 31 Mawrth 2005
Penderfyniad Mae 3 phenderfyniad posib:
  • mae dy gartref yn aros yn yr un band
  • mae dy gartref yn symud i fand is
  • mae dy gartref yn symud i fand uwch
Ar ôl y penderfyniad Mae’r penderfyniad yn derfynol, a dwyt ti ddim yn gallu gofyn am adolygiad arall.
