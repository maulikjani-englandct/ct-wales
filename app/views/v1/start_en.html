Council Tax

Use this service to:

  • find out the Council Tax band of any property in Wales
  • check the information we hold on your property
  • let us know if you think your Council Tax band is wrong

Who can use this service

Anyone can use this service to view the Council Tax band of properties in Wales.

If you want to let us know if any of the information about a property is wrong, you must be responsible for the Council Tax.

This can include those who:

  • own or rent a property
  • do not pay Council Tax (for example, students or people on benefits)

More information

The Welsh Government are making Council Tax fairer.

They will:

  • review current Council Tax bands
  • improve the process to change your Council Tax band (appeal)
  • share more about what Council Tax money is spent on
  • make it easier to see the information we hold on your property
