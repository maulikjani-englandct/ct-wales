I think my Council Tax band is wrong

This page is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

There are two ways you can request a review of your Council Tax band if you think it is wrong.

Formal challenge (making a proposal)

We are required by law to review your band if you ask us to. This is called a formal challenge and is also known as making a proposal.

You can make a proposal if:

  • you have been paying Council Tax on your property for less than six months
  • the VOA has changed your Council Tax band in the last six months

You can also make a proposal to tell us that your property has changed. For example:

  • the property has been demolished
  • it has been divided into new properties
  • it has been merged into a single property
  • there has been a physical change in your local area that has affected the value of your property

There’s no time limit on these examples – you can do it at any time.

If you’re making a proposal, it’s helpful if you can provide supporting evidence.

Informal challenge (band review)

If you think your band is wrong and you’ve been paying Council Tax for more than six months, you can request a band review.

Band reviews are known as informal challenges, and you can ask for one at any time. For band reviews, you must provide evidence to support your request for a review. The quality of evidence you provide is key.

We must see strong supporting evidence to accept a band review request. Without it, we will not be able to review your band.

Evidence to support a band review might include addresses of up to five properties in a lower Council Tax band than yours, if these properties are like yours in age, style, design, size and type.

They will need to be in a nearby street or estate if you live in a town or city, or within 10 miles of your property if you live in the countryside. We’ll consider a wider distance if you live in a rural area.

You can also use evidence from house prices if your property or similar properties were sold between 1 April 2001 and 31 March 2005 in Wales.

Rydw i'n meddwl bod fy mand Treth Gyngor yn anghywir

Mae'r dudalen hon hefyd ar gael yn Saesneg (English).

Mae dau ffordd i ti ofyn i'r VOA adolygu dy fand Treth Gyngor os wyt ti'n meddwl ei fod yn anghywir.

Her ffurfiol (gwneud cynnig)

Yn ôl y gyfraith, mae'n rhaid i'r VOA adolygu dy fand os wyt ti'n gofyn. Mae hyn yn cael ei alw'n her ffurfiol, neu 'gwneud cynnig'.

Rwyt ti'n gallu gwneud cynnig os:

  • wyt ti wedi bod yn gyfrifol am dalu y Dreth Gyngor ar dy gartref am lai na chwe mis
  • mae'r VOA wedi newid dy fand Treth Gyngor yn ystod y chwe mis diwethaf

Rwyt ti hefyd yn gallu gwneud cynnig os yw dy gartref wedi newid.

Er enghraifft:

  • mae'r cartref wedi cael ei ddymchwel
  • mae'r cartref wedi cael ei rannu'n gartrefi newydd
  • mae sawl cartref wedi cael eu cyfuno'n un cartref
  • mae rhywbeth wedi newid yn yr ardal leol sy'n effeithio ar werth dy gartref

Does dim terfyn amser ar yr enghreifftiau yma – rwyt ti'n gallu gwneud cynnig unrhyw bryd.

Os wyt ti'n gwneud cynnig, mae'n help os wyt ti'n gallu rhoi tystiolaeth i gefnogi beth wyt ti'n ei ddweud.

Her anffurfiol (adolygiad o fand)

Os wyt ti'n meddwl bod dy fand yn anghywir ac rwyt ti wedi bod yn talu Treth Gyngor am dros chwe mis, rwyt ti'n gallu gofyn am adolygiad o'r band.

Mae adolygiad o fand hefyd yn cael ei alw'n 'her anffurfiol', ac rwyt ti'n gallu gofyn am un unrhyw bryd.

Os wyt ti'n gofyn am adolygiad o fand, mae'n rhaid i ti roi tystiolaeth i ddangos pam rwyt ti'n meddwl bod y band yn anghywir.

Mae ansawdd y dystiolaeth rwyt ti'n rhoi i ni yn bwysig. Heb dystiolaeth dda, fydd y VOA ddim yn gallu adolygu dy fand.

Gall tystiolaeth gynnwys cyfeiriadau hyd at bum cartref mewn band is na dy gartref di, os yw'r cartrefi hynny'n debyg i dy gartref di o ran math, maint, oedran a steil.

Mae'n rhaid iddyn nhw fod ar stryd neu ystâd ar bwys dy gartref di os wyt ti'n byw mewn tref neu ddinas, neu o fewn 10 milltir os wyt ti'n byw yng nghefn gwlad.

Rwyt ti hefyd yn gallu defnyddio tystiolaeth prisiau tai os cafodd dy gartref neu gartrefi tebyg eu gwerthu rhwng 1 Ebrill 2001 a 31 Mawrth 2005.
