More information

What is Council Tax?

Council Tax is a tax on every home in England and Wales. This money helps pay for things like schools, rubbish collection, emergency services and social care.

Your local council works out how much tax is payable for each Council Tax band every year. They will send you your Council Tax bill and collect payment.

What is a Council Tax Band?

Every home is put into a group called a “band” by the Valuation Office Agency (VOA). Bands range from A to I in Wales, and band A is the lowest.

Find out more about band ranges.

The band is based on how much your home was worth on 1 April 2003 in Wales. The VOA is responsible for valuing your home and will look at what homes were selling for at that time, not what they are worth today.

To do this the VOA will look at the type, size and location of a property. If your home did not sell around these dates, or if it hadn’t been built then, the VOA will look at sales of similar homes which sold nearby.
